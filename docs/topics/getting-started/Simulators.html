Simulators

I'm sure you are as eager as I was to start flying, but before you spend a lot of money, please consider trying out a simulator first! Simulators are a cheap to see if the sport is something you like, and they're great for honing your skill. You can crash your drone hundreds of times in a simulator, and it won't cost you anything extra!

Simulators require a controller of some sort, though! While you can use an X-Box or PlayStation controller, it is strongly advised to buy a proper transmitter that you can connect to your PC. This ensures that you won't learn bad habits with the game controller, and gets you familiar with the transmitter/controller that you'll actually use to fly a real drone.

Popular simulators include:

  • LiftOff
  • Velocidrone
  • Drone Racing League
